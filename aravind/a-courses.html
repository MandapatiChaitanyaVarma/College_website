Junior College Programs at Sanjeev Junior College:
Sanjeev Junior College offers integrated Intermediate (10+2) programs that combine traditional academic curricula with specialized coaching for various competitive exams. These programs are designed to provide students with a strong foundation for higher education and professional careers.

MPC + IPMAT:
 Subjects: Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry
Integrated Coaching: IPMAT (Integrated Program in Management Aptitude Test)
Ideal For: Students aiming for careers in engineering and management.

MPC + CLAT:
 Subjects: Mathematics, Physics, Chemistry
Integrated Coaching: CLAT (Common Law Admission Test)
Ideal For: Students interested in pursuing law after a science background.

MEC + IPMAT/CLAT
Subjects: Mathematics, Economics, Commerce
Integrated Coaching: IPMAT and CLAT
Ideal For: Commerce students targeting management or law careers.

CEC + CA-CMA:
 Subjects: Civics, Economics, Commerce
Integrated Coaching: CA (Chartered Accountancy) and CMA (Cost and Management Accounting)
Ideal For: Students aspiring to become Chartered Accountants or Cost Accountants.
