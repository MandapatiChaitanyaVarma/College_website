Flashmob

Our flash mob is a surprise dance performance by our talented students, staged in common areas like the college courtyard or entrance. It begins unannounced- a few students start dancing, and suddenly, more join in, forming a lively, synchronized performance that leaves the crowd cheering.
The excitement builds in seconds, and before anyone can fully absorb the moment, the performance ends and the group disperses- leaving behind smiles, applause, and curiosity about whats coming next.
At Sanjeev, our flash mobs are not just about dance- they are about celebration, spirit, and unity.




Freshers Event

At Sanjeev College of Arts and Science, the arrival of new students is celebrated with great enthusiasm and warmth through our Freshers Event. This special occasion is dedicated to welcoming the fresh batch of students into our vibrant college community.
The Freshers Event is a fun-filled program where seniors and faculty come together to greet and encourage the newcomers.
